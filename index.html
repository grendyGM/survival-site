Survival

Timekeeping

A day is divided into six 4-hour Periods:

Day Night
Dawnrise (4 AM - 8 AM) Gloaming (4 PM - 8 PM)
Morning (8 AM - 12 PM) Nightfall (8 PM - 12 AM)
Mid-Day (12 PM - 4 PM) Midnight (12 AM - 4 AM)

Resource Management

  • Your supply of food, water, arrows, and torches are each tracked using a Resource Die (D6 to D12).
  • Every time you use one of these items, roll. On a 1 or 2, decrease the Resource Die one step. When you roll a D6 and get a 1 or 2, that item’s supply is depleted.
  • Resources can be replenished by foraging or crafting.
  • Each day, every PC must roll Resource Dice for both food and water.
    • Without food and water, a character gains Hunger or Thirst Exhaustion.
    • Some features allow characters to ignore food and water requirements.

Exhaustion

  • Exhaustion Track: 0–10, applying -1 to all d20 tests per level.
    • If a character fails an exhaustion check at 10 levels, the character dies.
  • Travel Exhaustion: Constitution saving throws.
    • Normal or Slow pace: No check required.
    • Fast pace: DC 13 (+3 per previous fast period), failure causes 1 level.
    • Traveling Overtime: DC 10 + 3 per extra period, failure causes 2 levels.
  • Hunger & Thirst:
    • Hunger: 1 level each day without 1 pound of food.
    • Thirst: 3 levels each day without one quart of water.
    • No save. Levels do not recover normally.
  • Recovery:
    • 1 period of rest restores 1 level.
    • Established Camp: +1 bonus level per Long Rest.
    • Comfortable lodging: +2 bonus levels per long rest.

Travel

Terrain & Travel Pace per Period

Terrain Slow Pace Normal Pace Fast Pace
Plains / Roads 1 hex (6 miles) 2 hex (12 miles) 3 hex (18 miles)
Forest / Hills 0.5 hex (3 miles) 1 hex (6 miles) 2 (12 miles)
Mountains / Swamps 0.3 hex (2 miles) 0.5 hex (3 miles) 1 (6 miles)

Encounter Checks

  • Roads: Encounter on 1–4.
  • Wilderness: Encounter on 1–5.

Travel Roles

  • Pathfinding:
    • Survival DC 13 in unexplored hexes.
    • Disadvantage in inclement weather.
  • Scout:
    • Perception or Stealth.
    • +10 over DC: Party gains Surprise.
    • Failure: Party is Surprised.
